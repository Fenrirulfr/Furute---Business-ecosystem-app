# AI Visibility Scoring Model

## Authority Signals – 30%
- **Backlinks**: High-quality links from reputable domains.
- **Mentions**: Citations and mentions in authoritative publications.
- **Social Proof**: Reviews, testimonials, and social media engagement.

## Structured Data – 20%
- **Schema Markup**: Implementation of relevant schemas (Organization, FAQPage, Article, etc.).
- **Rich Snippets**: Achieving rich results in search engine results pages.

## FAQ Density – 15%
- **Direct Answers**: Providing clear and concise answers to common questions.
- **FAQ Sections**: Including dedicated FAQ sections on key pages.

## Content Depth – 20%
- **Comprehensive Coverage**: Addressing topics in detail with relevant examples and data.
- **Semantic Relevance**: Using related keywords and entities naturally within the content.

## Internal Linking – 15%
- **Contextual Links**: Linking to relevant pages within the content to guide users and search engines.
- **Anchor Text**: Using descriptive anchor text for internal links.

## Target Score: 85+
- **Goal**: Achieve a score of 85 or higher across all metrics to maximize AI visibility.
